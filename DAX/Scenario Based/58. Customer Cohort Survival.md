## 58. **Customer Cohort Survival**  
    ```DAX
    Cohort Survival = 
    CALCULATE(
      [Total Sales], 
      FILTER(
        ALL('Date'), 
        'Date'[Date] >= MIN(Cohort[Start Date]) && 
        'Date'[Date] <= MAX(Cohort[End Date])
      )
    )
    ```
    *Tracks customer retention over custom periods.*
