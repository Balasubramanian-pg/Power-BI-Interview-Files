## 66. **Churn Prediction (No Purchases in 30 Days)**  
    ```DAX
    Churn Risk = 
    VAR LastPurchase = CALCULATE(MAX(Sales[Date]), ALL('Date'))
    RETURN
    IF(DATEDIFF(LastPurchase, TODAY(), DAY) > 30, "High Risk", "Low Risk")
    ```
